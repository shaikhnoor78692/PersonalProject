## LINKEDIN ALGORITHM KNOWLEDGE

What LinkedIn rewards:
1. Consistency — post 5-6x per week minimum
2. Engagement in first 60 mins — stay active after posting
3. Comments worth 10x more than likes
4. Reply to every comment within 1 hour
5. Personal story posts outperform promo 3:1
6. Native content preferred over shared links
7. 3-5 hashtags max
8. Connections see posts more than followers

What kills reach:
- External links in post body
- Posting and disappearing
- Only promotional content
- Irregular schedule

Best times to post (India):
- Morning: 8:00 AM – 9:30 AM
- Lunch: 12:00 PM – 1:00 PM
- Evening: 6:00 PM – 8:00 PM
