### Content Writing Order Process
```
Step 1: Client contacts via website form or Fiverr
Step 2: Ask client for:
        - Topic/title
        - Target audience
        - Keywords to include
        - Tone (professional/friendly)
        - Word count needed

Step 3: Open Claude.ai and paste:
        "Write a [word count] word SEO blog post 
        about [topic] for [business type] targeting 
        [audience]. Include keywords: [keywords]. 
        Tone: [professional/friendly]"

Step 4: Claude writes content in 2 minutes
Step 5: Review and edit if needed
Step 6: Send to client as Google Doc or Word file
Step 7: Client approves → payment received ✅
```
